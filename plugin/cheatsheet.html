Key Attributes

Fill & Stroke

  • fill="#3B82F6" - Fill color
  • stroke="#000" - Outline color
  • stroke-width="2" - Outline thickness
  • fill-opacity="0.8" - Transparency

Text Positioning

  • text-anchor="start|middle|end"
  • dominant-baseline="middle"
  • transform="rotate(45)"
